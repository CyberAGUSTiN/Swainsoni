Translation

The Halcón / Hawk Solution

The Western Hemisphere basically speaks two languages: English + Spanish. And the dynamic between them is better represent by the bird that gave us our name -the Swainson’s Hawk (in Spanish called Halcón de Swainson). At the beginning of every winter, it flies from Alaska, Canada and the USA to Argentina -and passing over all of South and Central America again, it goes back to the North, looking here for a new summer. Let others aspire to world conquest. Like the halcón / hawk, we will be content with the Western Hemisphere.
